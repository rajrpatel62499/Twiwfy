Make location adjustments

Whether you’re outdoors, in a public space, or indoors in a space you own, it’s likely you'll need to make some adjustments to the format of your experience.

  • If you are in public spaces, change your meeting location and the venues you visit in order to avoid crowds. Keep guests moving rather than being sedentary as the latter increases the risk for transmission.
  • If you’re indoors, consider limiting the areas a guest can enter in your home to only the necessary spaces.
  • Consider moving indoor experiences to the outdoors to make more space for social distancing.
