Minimum guests

Experiences on TWIWFY have a 1 guest minimum. That means if one person books your experience, you’ll still be expected to host them.

Service fees

Twiwfy takes 20% of each booking. TWIWFY handles payment processing, provides 24-hour customer service, and maintains liability insurance for most experiences. Read more about our service fee.

Exclusivity

Each experience date you schedule through Twiwfy must only be attended by Twiwfy guests. Guests who book and pay through other platforms must be hosted at separate times.
