How much will my friend get when I invite them?

When the friend you've invited to Twiwfy signs up and books a trip, how much they’ll get depends on the price of their reservation excluding taxes and guest fees:

Spend ₹24,000, get ₹1,200
Spend ₹40,000, get ₹2,000
Spend ₹56,000, get ₹2,800
Spend ₹80,000 or more, get ₹4,000

Plus, they’ll get ₹1,000 off when they book their first experience of ₹4,000 or more.
